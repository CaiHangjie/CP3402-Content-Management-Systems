Product Introduction

Tea has become an indispensable beverage in people's lives, with a cultural heritage of five thousand years. Looking back at the development history of tea, the book "The Classic of Tea" records that "tea originated from the Shennong family as a beverage." In order to treat the tribe members, Shennong personally tasted hundreds of herbs. During the process of tasting hundreds of herbs, he unexpectedly discovered that tea has the effect of detoxification. From then on, people began to plant and study it, and it has been passed down to today. Black tea is a type of tea with a relatively long history in China, belonging to fully fermented tea, red soup, and red leaves, so it is called "black tea". Black tea originated in Mount Wuyi, Fujian Province. The common black tea includes small black tea, kungfu black tea and broken black tea.
